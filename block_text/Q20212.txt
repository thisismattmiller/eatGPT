I should also say, the other thing about E.A.T.— You said, “How did you find the engineers?” There was an extraordinary response all over the country. People in different places either who knew Bob or knew Billy—or didn’t know them at all—loved the idea. They would say, “Can we start E.A.T. chapters?” We’d say, “Yes.” So in Boston, Philadelphia, Washington, friends began to start these chapters that began to do their own activities, attracting local engineers and local artists.
