THEY ARE MADE BY TAKING AN OBJECT AND VACUUM-FORMING THIN SHEETS (60-150 MIL) OF POLYSTYRENE AROUND IT. THE THIN POLYSTYRENE COLLAPSES AROUND THE OBJECT FORM IN FRACTIONS OF A SECOND AND CAN BE USED TO FORM SOFT OBJECTS SUCH AS GRAPES. POLY-EXPANDABLE STYRENE IS A DISPOSABLE MATERIAL NORMALLY USED TO PLATFORM-PACKAGE ITEMS LIKE RADIOS AND OTHER APPLIANCES.
